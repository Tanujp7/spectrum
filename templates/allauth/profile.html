Frequently Asked Questions

How many books can I rate?

You can rate as many books as you want. Please try and rate atleast 50 books.

On what basis should I rate the book?

"Don't judge the book by it's cover", that's exactly what we are not going to do. You have to make a judgement based on the topic, title, and description of the book. If you think a book seems really interesting to you then you may give a positive rating.

These books are not my type! Should I give them a negative rating?

Yes. Imagine you have the opportunity to pick few books for free. Which books would you choose?
You wouldn't want to read a book does not seem interesting to you.

Is it safe to give away my personal information?

Yes we use state of the art security mechanism and your information is safe with us. You can delete your account at any time. Just email us at recommendersystemprototype at gmail
